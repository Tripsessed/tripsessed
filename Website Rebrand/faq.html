Expeditions & impact travel

Impact travel is the practice of intentionally incorporating meaningful community experiences into your trip — visiting schools, supporting local initiatives, or contributing to on-the-ground projects. Through our partnership with United in One Global Foundation, we can connect your journey with communities around the world in ways that go far beyond the typical tourist experience.

No. Impact activities are always optional unless you're joining a dedicated impact expedition like our Nigeria or Kenya trips. For standard luxury travel, we simply offer the option to incorporate a community visit or contribution if you'd like. There's never any pressure.

United in One Global Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit with deep roots in Africa and a growing global presence, focused on education, clean water access, healthcare, and economic empowerment for communities in need. A portion of every Tripsessed booking supports their mission. You can learn more at unitedinone.org.

Our group expeditions are limited to 8 travelers to keep the experience intimate and personal.
