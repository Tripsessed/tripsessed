Who we are

Luxury travel, without the guesswork

Tripsessed was founded by sophisticated explorers who believe that how you travel is as important as where you go. We're not a booking engine — we're your personal advocates, obsessed with building experiences that are exclusive, authentic, and impossible to replicate on your own. And for those who want more than luxury, we offer something rarer: travel that gives back.

As FORA-affiliated advisors with access to Four Seasons Preferred Partner, Marriott STARS, and Hyatt Privé programs, we unlock perks that aren't available when you book direct.

  • Complimentary room upgrades (when available)
  • Daily breakfast for two at partner properties
  • Hotel and resort credits for dining, spa, and experiences
  • Early check-in and late check-out
  • VIP welcome amenities and personalized touches
  • Same price as booking direct — or better

Travel with purpose

See the world.
Change it too.

We've built a unique partnership with United in One Global Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit working across communities in need around the world — with deep roots in Africa and ongoing efforts spanning education, clean water, healthcare access, and economic empowerment.

This means your luxury trip can include a private visit to a community school, a clean water project, or a healthcare initiative — experiences that add a dimension to your journey that no five-star hotel can replicate. Whether you're heading to Africa or anywhere else in the world, we can help you weave purpose into the experience.
